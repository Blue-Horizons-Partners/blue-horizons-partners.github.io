## Information We Collect

**Information you provide to us:** We may collect information you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, and any other details you submit through contact forms or inquiries.

**Information collected automatically:** When you visit our website, we and our advertising partners automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing activity, including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring URLs.

**Information from Meta:** When you interact with our advertisements on Meta platforms (Facebook and Instagram), Meta may share aggregated and anonymized data with us about ad performance. We may use Meta Pixel or similar tracking technologies on our website, which allows Meta to collect information about your visit for the purpose of measuring ad effectiveness and delivering relevant advertising.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Operate and improve our website and services
- Respond to your inquiries and requests
- Measure the performance of our advertising campaigns, including those on Meta platforms
- Deliver relevant advertisements to you and others with similar interests via Meta's Custom Audiences and Lookalike Audiences features
- Comply with our legal obligations

## Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

- **Meta Platforms, Inc.:** As described above, we use Meta Pixel and related tools. Information collected through these tools is subject to [Meta's Data Policy](https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy/).
- **Service providers:** Third-party vendors who assist us in operating our website and business, bound by confidentiality obligations.
- **Legal requirements:** When required by law or to protect our rights.

## Meta Advertising and Your Choices

We use Meta's advertising tools, including the Meta Pixel, to understand how users interact with our website after seeing our ads, and to serve relevant ads to users on Facebook and Instagram. You can manage your ad preferences on Meta platforms by visiting your [Facebook Ad Preferences](https://www.facebook.com/ads/preferences/) or [Instagram Settings](https://www.instagram.com/accounts/privacy_and_security/).

You may also opt out of interest-based advertising through the [Digital Advertising Alliance](http://www.aboutads.info/choices/) or [Network Advertising Initiative](http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/).
